How it works
SPARQ is a composite athleticism score combining NFL Combine measurements — speed, explosion, strength, and agility — into a single number. Each player is then ranked by σ (sigma): how far above or below average they are for their position, relative to every NFL draftee since 1999. +2σ ELITE top ~2% of all-time. +1σ ABOVE AVG legitimately impressive. 0σ AVERAGE historical norm. −1σ BELOW AVG not a freak. A +2σ OT and a +2σ WR are equally rare for their position.  ·  Methodology & Source ↗
